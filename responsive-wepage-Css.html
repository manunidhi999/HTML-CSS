Users

Manage all registered users. Shows information about the people using your website/app.

  • Common Data Displayed
  • Total users
  • Active users today
  • New registrations
  • User growth %
    • The Users card represents information about the people who use the website or application. It usually displays details such as the total number of registered users, active users, or new sign-ups over a specific period. This card helps administrators quickly understand user growth and engagement and is commonly used in dashboards, admin panels, and company portals to manage and monitor users efficiently.

    Sales

    Track monthly sales performance. Shows business performance in terms of money. Common Data Displayed.

  • Total sales amount
  • Monthly revenue
  • Daily earnings
  • Profit / loss percentage
    • The Sales card focuses on the financial performance of the system or business. It typically shows data such as total revenue, daily or monthly sales, and growth or decline percentages compared to previous periods. This card is widely used in e-commerce platforms and business dashboards to give decision-makers a quick overview of how well the business is performing.

    Reports

    View analytics and reports. Gives access to analytics and performance reports.Common Data Displayed.

  • Number of reports generated
  • System activity
  • Usage statistics
  • Download count
    • The Reports card provides access to analytics and performance insights. It often summarizes the number of available reports, recent activity, or the last update time and serves as an entry point to detailed charts, graphs, or downloadable data. This card helps users analyze trends, monitor system performance, and make informed decisions based on data.
